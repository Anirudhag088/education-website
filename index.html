Frequently Asked Questions

What types of resources are available on your website?

We offer a wide range of free resources and crash courses for your entrance exam like jee,neet including boards exams.

Is there any cost or subscription for accessing the study materials?

No, all of our study materials are completely free and can be accessed without any cost.

Are the free study materials suitable for all levels of learners?

Yes, our free study materials are designed for learners of all levels, from beginners to advanced students.

Is there a limit on how many free study materials I can access?

No, there is no limit on the number of free study materials that you can access on our website.

What kind of educational counselling services do you offer?

Our experienced counsellors can help students with everything from choosing the right college or university to applying for financial aid. They can also provide guidance on study habits and time management, as well as offer advice on how to deal with stress and anxiety related to school.

Who is eligible to receive educational counselling services?

Our counselling services are available to students currently studying in class 11th and 12th.

Who are the education counselors on your website?

Our education counselors are experts and top performers in their fields, including successful graduates, industry professionals, and experienced educators.

How can I schedule an education counseling session?

You can schedule an education counseling session by visiting our website and selecting the appropriate counselor or package. You can also contact us for more information.
